New Jersey

In my quest to visit all the states of America, it may come as a surprise to many that the state that I have visited most frequently is indeed the place where that godawful TV show Jersey Shore comes from. The reason for this is that a couple of my good friends live in NJ, and thus its my curse to fly over there and visit them at semi-regular intervals.
