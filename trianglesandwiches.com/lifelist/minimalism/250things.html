Own Less than 250 Things

One of my big goals for 2011 is to get really really simple. The most obvious way to start doing this is in my physical possessions. By the end of Jan 2011, I wanted to have reduced everything I own to under 250 items. (Caveat: This does not include any replenishable items like toiletries, stationery etc)

Back in December, I moved house, and I took that opportunity to really cut back on 75% of my stuff. I recycled, threw away, gave away, and sold most of my possessions, and it felt GREAT! After doing a bit more cutting back since moving into my new place, I managed to hit under the magical 250 item mark!

Inspired by Colin Wright of Exile Lifestyle fame, I decided to photodocument all my possessions, as a nice way of visually tracking my progress as I move further and further towards hitting under 100 items.

Another caveat: things that I have not taken pictures of but have included in a final count are:

  • the clothes in my laundry basket (14 items)
  • underwear (because, seriously?!) (33 items)
  • my car (1 item)
  • the 2 household items that I own in the whole world - my bowl and my wok (2 items)
